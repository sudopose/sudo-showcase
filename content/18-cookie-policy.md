### 5. What is a "Cookie"?
A cookie is a small text file stored on your device (computer, mobile phone) when you visit a website. It allows the website to remember your preferences and activity during your session or upon return visits. Cookies are passive and cannot contain or execute malicious software.

---

### 6. What are the Advantages of Cookies?
Cookies make your browsing experience smoother and help websites function efficiently by remembering login details, language preferences, and other settings.

---

### 7. Cookie Lifespan
Cookies can be either:
- **Session cookies**, which are deleted when you close your browser
- **Persistent cookies**, which remain stored on your device for a set period or until manually deleted
